X-Ray Services

An X-ray lab plays a crucial role in medical diagnostics, using advanced imaging technology to visualize bones, organs, and tissues. It helps detect fractures, infections, and abnormalities like tumors. Quick, non-invasive, and highly accurate.

Eye Services

An eye lab specializes in diagnosing and treating vision issues through advanced tests and imaging, ensuring accurate assessments for conditions like glaucoma, cataracts, and refractive errors.

Clinical diagnostic Services
